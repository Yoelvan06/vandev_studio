Get ready for the battle

Rock paper scissors is a really fun minigame that we do with a lot of people. You know how to play it, you know how to win it. But don't you think its a little bit too boring when you do it so many times. Not anymore, i'm going to challange you to play baguntas battle. baguntas is the weird name that i pick, sorry for that. Baguntas is a game about playing rock paper scissors but with style. Here you have over 20 characters and stages that ready to be challanged for fight. However, they're not doing any different neither the gameplay. They're just there standing there and doing exactly nothing, trying to be honest here.

Why do i call it baguntas? If you translate Rock paper scissors to indonesia, its called "Batu gunting kertas" Combine all the words and you get yourself Baguntas. The main point is, You have yourself a rock paper scissors fighting game.
